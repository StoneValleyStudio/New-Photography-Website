About Will

A passion for sport.
A gift for storytelling.

Will Morris has spent years on the sidelines, courtside, and poolside — not as a spectator, but as a storyteller with a lens. He understands sport from the inside: the nerves before the whistle, the split-second of contact, the tearful celebration after the final buzzer.

Whether you're a high school athlete working toward a scholarship, a youth league player finding their game, or a parent who wants to hold onto these years forever — Will's photography creates something real, something lasting, something yours.
